Research Interests

My research focuses on how biomolecular condensates control cellular physiology and plant development. I integrate liquid–liquid phase separation (LLPS) assays, plant biology models, and single-molecule imaging techniques to link molecular interactions with mesoscale organization.

  • Condensate formation & material states: in vitro reconstitution, turbidity/FRAP, partitioning, client recruitment.
  • Plant signaling & physiology: GI/ELF3/FKF1 circadian pathways, chloroplast–nucleus communication, senescence regulation.
  • Single-molecule approaches: smFISH, MS2 live tracking, quantitative spot analysis to connect transcriptional bursts to condensate dynamics.
